Development and Growth Process

We manage every part of your Golf YouTube channel growth, from strategy to publishing and analytics.

1.

Fill out the channel details form

You begin by filling a short form that helps us understand your goals, audience style and the type of golf content you create. You can keep it simple or share everything in detail. Once submitted, our team reviews your answers and prepares the next step so we can start building your growth plan.

2.

We create your Channel Growth Blueprint

After reviewing your form, we prepare a customized growth blueprint for your golf channel. This includes positioning, keyword opportunities, topic direction, upload structure, CTR improvements and an overall strategy designed to help your channel grow consistently.

3.

Strategy briefing and discovery phase

Once the blueprint is confirmed, our strategists begin a detailed discovery phase. They study past videos, thumbnails, titles, retention patterns and golf niche competitors. This helps us identify what is slowing your growth and what can unlock faster views and returning viewers.

4.

Receive your content strategy and topic lineup

Within a few days, you receive your upcoming content plan. This includes topic ideas, optimized titles, thumbnail concepts, keyword clusters and upload recommendations built for the golf niche. You only need to review and approve the plan before we begin managing uploads.

5.

We optimize every upload you publish

For each scheduled upload, our team handles title optimization, thumbnail direction, SEO descriptions, keyword placement, playlist mapping and publishing recommendations. You simply share the video and we prepare everything around it for maximum reach on YouTube.

6.

Ongoing weekly management and optimization

Your channel grows through continuous execution. We monitor CTR, retention, traffic sources and viewer behavior, adjust topics based on performance, update older videos for new traffic and improve thumbnails and titles regularly so your channel keeps moving forward.

7.

Access your Notion dashboards and updates

You receive access to your own Notion workspace and Google Sheets that track your content calendar, optimization tasks, analytics reviews, titles, thumbnails and progress updates. Everything is organized and easy to follow so you always know what is happening with your golf channel.
